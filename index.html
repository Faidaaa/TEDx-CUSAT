TEDxCUSAT

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group.

This year's theme: Kaleidoscope. Just as a kaleidoscope forms beautiful, ever-changing patterns from fragmented pieces of glass, our perspectives shape how we see the world. We are bringing together diverse voices that will challenge, inspire, and shift your realities.
